Who We Are

Metadata Technologies was incorporated in 2002 with the vision of helping businesses build strong customer relationships. We focus on providing streamlined
Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Solutions across the world. The idea has always been to deliver cost-effective solutions without deviating from quality. We
provide top-notch services by leveraging Microsoft Dynamics 365 CRM.

What is Metadata Technologies?

A trusted partner providing in-depth CRM
consulting & support to streamline business
operations through the Microsoft Platform.

Why Us?

Two decades of domain expertise in CRM,
providing quality services to corporate entities
around the globe across segments.

What sets us apart?

We are much more than just a software solution
provider. Metadata partners with you to provide
holistic solutions from start to finish.
